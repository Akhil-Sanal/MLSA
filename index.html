About MLSA LBSCEK

MLSA (Microsoft Learn Student Ambassadors) is an MNC-approved global student community directly run by Microsoft, one of the world's biggest multinational tech companies.

  • You're officially part of a Microsoft-recognized program.
  • Direct resources, mentorship, and recognition from Microsoft.
  • Your title, achievements, and events are backed by Microsoft, adding credibility to your profile.
  • Official Microsoft Learn Student Ambassador title on LinkedIn & CV — globally recognized.
